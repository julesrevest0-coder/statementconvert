Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service: files you upload for conversion, and basic usage data (no personal information required to use the free tier).

Your files are used solely to extract transaction data and return it to you. We do not read, store, sell or share your financial data with any third party.

All uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 60 minutes of upload. No exceptions.

We use minimal cookies for analytics (page views only). No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies.
